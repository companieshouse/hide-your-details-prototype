Apply to remove personal details from Companies House public documents

Use this service to ask for personal details such as a home address to be removed from publicly available documents on the Companies House register.

You can do this if the home address has been used as a correspondence address, or as a company's former registered office address.

If the home address is being used as the company's current registered office address, you must submit a filing to update the registered office address first.

Before you start

As part of this service, you'll need to tell us which documents you want us to remove the home address from. It costs £XX for each document you select.

Find out which documents contain the home address by searching for the company on the Companies House register and checking its filing history.

To use this service, you'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • the company number
  • the applicant’s name, date of birth and any previous names used on the Companies House register
  • the home address you want to remove
  • an email address we can contact if we need more information
  • a credit or debit card

If the home address is being used as someone's correspondence address, you'll also need to provide an address to replace it.

It takes around 10 minutes to complete this application.
