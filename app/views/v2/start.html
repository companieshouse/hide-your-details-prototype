Apply to remove personal details from Companies House public documents

Use this service to ask for personal details to be removed from historical documents on the Companies House public register. This includes:

  • home address
  • signature
  • full date of birth
  • business occupation
If the home address is being used as the company's current registered office address, you must submit a filing to update the registered office address first.

Before you start

As part of this service, you'll need to tell us which documents you want us to remove personal details from. It costs £XX for each document you select.

Find out which documents contain the home address by searching for the company on the Companies House register and checking its filing history.

To use this service, you'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • the company number
  • the applicant’s full name, date of birth and any previous names used on the Companies House register
  • the home address you want to remove, if applicable
  • an email address we can contact if we need more information
  • a credit or debit card

If the home address is being used as someone's correspondence address, you may also need to provide an address to replace it.

It takes around 10 minutes to complete this application.

Other ways to remove your personal information

If you only want to change the correspondence address that appears in the 'people' tab on the company's public record, you can file a change to company information for free.

If you're at serious risk of violence or intimidation, or your details have been used by a company without your permission, read the guidance on removing your personal information from the Companies House public register.
